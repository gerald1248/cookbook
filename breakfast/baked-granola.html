    Baked granola

    Ingredients

    500g big oats 70g coconut oil 90g golden sirup 2 Tbs vanilla extract 2 tsp cinnamon 1/2 tsp salt

    Stir while pouring in the melted oil and sirup.

    Bake at 150-160C for 40 mins. Stir halfway through, after 20 mins. Bake in 2cm layer.
