# Vegan Mett

<!--See [www.zuckerjagdwurst.com](https://www.zuckerjagdwurst.com/de/rezepte/veganes-mett).-->

## Ingredients
100g rice cakes

250-300ml water

1/2-1 white onion

50g tomato paste

Salt & pepper

2 sprigs parsley

4 white bread rolls

## Preparation
Crumble the rice cakes in a bowl with your fingers.

Add the water and gradually fold in with a fork so that the water can distribute well. Let soak.

Cut off the ends of the onions, remove the peel and dice the onion.

Add diced onion and tomato paste to the soaked rice cake and fold in with a fork and distribute well.

Season well with salt and pepper. Then let rest in the refrigerator for a few hours for best results.
