# Kidney Bean Smoked Tofu Leberwurst Vegan

Taken from: [cheapandcheerfulcooking](https://cheapandcheerfulcooking.com)

## Ingredients
1 can kidney beans, 240g drained weight
150g smoked tofu
1 onion, small
2 tsp marjoram
1 tsp mustard
1 tsp smoked salt, alternatively regular salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp vegetable oil, e.g. rapeseed oil

## Preparation
1. Finely dice the onion and saut'e in a pan with a dash of vegetable oil at medium temperature for  a few minutes. Add marjoram and sweat briefly. Remove from the heat and let cool down a bit.

2. Drain the beans well. Roughly crumble the tofu. Mix with all the other ingredients in a food processor or with the help of a hand blender until the vegan liver sausage has the desired consistency. If necessary, add a few drops of water in the course of this.
