# Soya burgers

## Ingredients 

210g soya mince, finely minced (eg Linda McCartney frozen) *
70g onions, finely minced almost puréed 
1 tsp garlic, finely minced almost puréed
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp ketchup / tomato purée 
2 Tbs flat leaved parsley, finely minced
1.5 Tbs chickpea flower **

\* (try adding finely minced and fried mushrooms)
\*\* (try poha thick rice flakes instead or a mix)


## Method
Mix all ingredients, form patties and let them cool in the fridge.
