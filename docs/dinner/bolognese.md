# Bolognese

## Ingredients 

1 Tbsp olive oil
500g (red) onions, finely minced
6 cloves of garlic
120g celery, finely minced 
120g carrots, finely minced
120g optional: courgette / red pepper, finely minced
1 tsp olive oil 
400g mushrooms, finely minced
750g soya mince (frozen, Linda McCartney)
1 Tbs yeast extract
2 Tbs soya sauce / tamari
2 tsp (smoked) salt
4 tins chopped tomatoes
1 bay leave
2 Tbs tomato purée 
1 Tbsp sugar
1(?) tsp salt (season to taste w salt after cooking)

## Method
Fry onions in olive oil until ever so slightly browned. Add garlic for 1 minute in the end. Fry veggies for 3 minutes. Transfer to slow cooker.
Add the tsp of olive oil then fry mushrooms until all water has evaporated. Add soya mince and continue frying until defrosted and slightly fried. Season with yeast extract, soya sauce and smoked salt. Mix thoroughly. Transfer to the slow cooker.
Pour tinned tomatoes in the pot used for frying and mash with a masher before adding them to the slow cooker. Add tomato purée and sugar, mix thoroughly. Hide bay leave near the centre.

Cook on high for 5h.

Season with salt / black pepper to taste after cooking.
