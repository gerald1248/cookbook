# Cajun kidney beans

## Ingredients
1Tbs oil
400g minced red onions
30g minced garlic
2 Tbs Cajun spice mix (M&S)
400g finely minced green peppers
400g finely minced celery (peeled on the outside)
10 tins Kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
2 bay leaves
450ml veg broth
3.5 tsp smoked salt / salt
2 tsp dark brown sugar
3 Tbs lime juice

## Method
* Fry onions until browned
* Add garlic and spices and fry some more
* Add everything to the slow cooker cook on high (hours) or on low (hours)
* Mash about half the kidney beans, just inside the slow cooker.
