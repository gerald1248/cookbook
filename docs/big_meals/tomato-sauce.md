# Tomato sauce creamy with vegetables 

## Ingredients 
1-2 Tbs olive oil
200g onions, chopped 
1-2 Tbsp garlic, minced
100-200g vegetables, minced (carrot and celery or courgette / sweet peppers)kkk
2 tins chopped tomatoes 
6 sundried tomatoes, chopped 
2 tsp sugar 
1.5 tsp salt

## Method
Fry onions in olive oil until ever so slightly browned. Add garlic and veggies for 2 mins, stir a few times. Pour in tinned tomatoes and sundried tomatoes. Let simmer for 30-60mins. Transfer to a high speed blender and blend smooth. Season with 1.5 tsp salt and 2 tsp sugar.

For creaminess add cashews and water 2:1 for the high speed blender step.
