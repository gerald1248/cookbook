# Carrot soup (creamy Thai)

## Ingredients

1 Tbsp neutral oil

250g onions, chopped

1.5 Tbsp garlic, minced

2 Tbsp ginger, minced

650g carrots 1.5cm

350g parsnip (or more carrot)

1l broth, veg or (fake) chicken

2 Tbsp soya sauce / tamari

2 Tbsp vegetarian fish sauce

0.5-1 Tbs sriracha 

50g creamed coconut (optional)

250ml of orange juice

2 Tbsp sugar

## Method

Fry the onions until golden. Add garlic and ginger and fry for another minute. Add carrots and broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 8 mins. Add parsnip cubes and simmer for another 18 minutes. Add sauces and sugar, blend smooth in high speed blender. Pour back into the pot and stir in orange juice.
