# Potato Fritters

## Ingredients
750g potatoes, grated
2tsp fine salt
2tsp onion powder 
2tsp onion granules 
3 heaped Tbsp gram flour + 9Tbsp warm water (replaces 3 eggs)
35g oat flour

## Method
Grate potatoes. Mix w 2 tsp fine salt. Transfer mix to fine sieve and place sieve over bowl. Let liquid drain, push liquid out w a large spoon and applying pressure. Once most liquid has drained, tip out liquid and put grated and salted potatoes back in the bowl.

Add onion powder and granules plus gram eggs. Mix well. Add oat flour. Mix some more and let rest for 15 mins.
Fry in small portions (slightly heaped Tbsp) from both sides medium heat
