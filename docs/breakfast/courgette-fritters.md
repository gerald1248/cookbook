# Courgette fritters

## Ingredients
500g courgettes, grated
2tsp fine salt
2tsp onion powder 
2tsp onion granules 
1 heaped Tbsp gram flour + 3Tbsp warm water (replaces 1 eggs)
30g potato starch
25g oat flour

## Method
Grate courgettes. Mix w 2 tsp fine salt. Transfer mix to fine sieve and place sieve over bowl. Let liquid drain, push liquid out w a large spoon and applying pressure. Once most liquid has drained, tip out liquid and put grated and salted courgettes back in the bowl.

Add gram egg. Mix well. Mix onion powder, granules, potato starch and 
oat flour. Add the dry mix to the bowl. Mix some more and let rest for 15 mins.

Fry in small portions (slightly heaped Tbsp) from both sides medium heat
